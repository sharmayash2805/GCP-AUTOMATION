The Challenge

  • ✗ Manual & slow land allocation
  • ✗ Opaque verification (high fraud risk)

  • ✗ No unified national reporting

The Solution

  • ✓ Automated GIS-based allocation
  • ✓ Real-time IoT & Satellite Monitoring
  • ✓ AI-driven verification engine

⚙️ End-to-End Automated Pipeline

1

Land Allocation & Registration

Companies view GIS-mapped degraded lands. Apply for >5ha parcels. System checks overlaps and locks parcel upon ICFRE approval.

2

IoT Baseline & Plantation

Sensors deployed (Moisture, Soil C). Satellite baseline captured. Company commences ecological restoration activities.

3

AI Continuous Monitoring

Satellites (Sentinel-2) capture 6-monthly imagery. AI Engine processes NDVI, canopy density, and biomass indices.

4

Smart Verification Trigger

If AI confidence score >85% and minimum thresholds met (e.g., 60% canopy), system auto-flags parcel and dispatches Field Auditor.

5

Credit Issuance & Redemption

Multi-metric calculation generates Green Credits. Deposited to company's immutable ledger. Redeemable for ITR/CSR/Subsidies.

The Scoring Engine

Ecological Performance Score (EPS)
EPS = (Canopy% × W₁) + (SoilCarbon × W₂) + (BioIndex × W₃)
Final Credit Formula
Total GC = Area (ha) × CpH × EPS × VCM

CpH: Credits-per-Hectare coefficient
VCM: Verification Confidence Multiplier (AI + Audit concordance)
