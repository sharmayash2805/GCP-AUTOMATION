🌐 Unified Map Grid

The core architectural innovation. The system automatically fetches APIs from Environment, Land, and Forestry ministries, mapping them into a unified spatial grid (PostGIS) to precisely identify unallocated, degraded lands suitable for plantation.

Automated Verification Lifecycle

1

Land Application

Company applies for >5ha grid slot. Locked upon approval.

2

IoT & Baseline

Sensors deployed. Initial satellite topography captured.

3

AI Verification

6-monthly NDVI & canopy density analysis via AI Engine.

4

Credit Calculation

If confidence >85%, credits issued to immutable ledger.

Credit Engine Algorithm

EPS = (Canopy% × W1) + (BioIndex × W2)
Total_GC = Area_ha × CpH × EPS × VCM
