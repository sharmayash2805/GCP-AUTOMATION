⚠️ The Current Challenge

Currently, restoring degraded land involves slow, manual processes. Companies struggle to find available land, manual verifications by officers are prone to delays and errors, and earning green credits can take up to 2 years, discouraging participation.

💡 Our Vision & Solution

A fully digital, transparent platform that automates land allocation and uses Satellite Imagery & AI to track forest growth. By removing red tape, we guarantee fast, fraud-free credit issuance, boosting India's green cover rapidly.

How the Platform Works

🗺️

1. Discover & Apply

Companies browse a digital map of degraded government lands and apply online instantly.

🌱

2. Plant & Restore

The land is locked for the company. They begin planting trees and restoring the local ecology.

Automated
🛰️

3. AI Monitoring

Satellites take pictures from space every 6 months. AI automatically measures canopy growth and health.

🏆

4. Earn Credits

Once AI verifies the forest has grown, digital Green Credits are issued to the company's wallet.
