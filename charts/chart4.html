🔄 Operational Flowchart

1

Registration & GIS Discovery

Companies browse the unified digital map of degraded lands. Minimum 5ha plots are selected and application is submitted.

2

Allocation & IoT Baseline

Land is locked to prevent overlap. Ground IoT sensors are installed and an initial satellite baseline is captured.

3

Continuous AI Remote Sensing

Automated

Every 6 months, AI analyzes satellite data to calculate canopy density, NDVI score, and forest growth progression.

4

Smart Trigger & Audit

If AI confidence >85% and ecological thresholds are met, a Field Auditor is automatically dispatched for final physical verification.

5

Credit Issuance & Ledgering

Digital green credits are calculated and deposited into the corporate ledger, ready for CSR, ITR, or subsidy redemption.
